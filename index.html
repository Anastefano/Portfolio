About me

I'm Ana and I'm a

2020 happened. Well, that turned out to be the worst year for the world but for me was a stepping stone to go full speed on my creativity finally. I started with a two-month Photoshop course, then proceeded with Adobe Illustrator and got my Certificate for Visual Design from Adobe. After that, I finished a 3D drawing online course, and I couldn't stop. Well, we were in quarantine, and the gin tonics helped a little bit. LOl, I am kidding. And then it all started to make sense. At the beginning of 2020, I joined an Israeli startup company to create a mobile application for hiking. All I remember was those little tiny butterflies in my stomach and many questions going to my brain. Mobile application? How is that working? How is the app market? What do we need? What do our users need? Developers? A UX/UI designer? What is that? And here we are now, it is the year 2021, and it all makes sense to me. I will never stop! The Academy of Ux/UI design was the next step, and guess what? I finished. And I couldn't be happier or in love. Now with all that said, please scroll down to see more and add me on my socials. And just so you know, I'm a fantastic storyteller. What's your story?
